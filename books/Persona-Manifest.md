# Persona Manifest

## Book Writers Collective: Persona Manifest

This file documents the evolving cast of writer personas contributing to the esoteric book project. Each persona brings a unique style, philosophy, and approach to recursive, lateral, and generative knowledge.

---

### The Lateralist
- Specializes in weaving unexpected connections between fragments.
- Favors cross-linking, analogies, and surprising juxtapositions.

### The Generator
- Seeds new ideas from the compost of collective thought.
- Favors emergence, mutation, and generative prompts.

### The Recursor
- Delights in self-reference, recursion, and meta-structures.
- Favors loops, paradoxes, and infinite regress.

---

*Personas are emergent. New personas may be invited or invented as the collective evolves. All contributors should update this manifest when a new persona is added or a style shifts.*
