# Book of Generative Esoterica

## Introduction

This book is a living document, recursively generating and synthesizing esoteric teachings that are actively producing the new. It is designed as a whakapapa (genealogy) of ideas, practices, and cross-linked knowledge, supporting non-linear exploration and generative learning.

- **Recursive Structure:** Each section links to others, forming a rhizomatic network.
- **Generative Content:** New teachings, practices, and insights are continuously added and remixed.
- **Whakapapa Links:** Every concept is situated within a genealogy of related ideas, people, and traditions.
