The Constraint Poets: On the Beauty of Limits

A sonnet about memory layout and the joy of boundaries:

    In lines and grids, our freedom finds its form,
    Constraint, the mother of invention's spark.
    We rhyme in bytes, in cycles, in the norm,
    And find the infinite inside the mark.
