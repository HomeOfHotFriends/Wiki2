# Recursive Prompts

Recursive Prompts are questions, koans, and dialogue forms that generate further inquiry and knowledge. Each prompt is linked to its whakapapa and cross-synthesized with practices and teachings.

## Example Prompts
- "What is the whakapapa of this idea?"
- "How does this practice generate the new?"
- "Where do these teachings intersect?"

> "A prompt is a seed for recursion."
