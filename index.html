Rhizomatic Design

He hurihanga HTML o ngā kōwae whakataki, kua whakamāoritia ia kōwae hei motu kōrero motuhake. Kāore ngā whiti e herea kinotia ki a rātou anō, engari ka tupu takitahi, ka kōrero takitahi, ka peka takitahi.

Section 0: Introductions

I whakamātau ahau ki te tuhi pukapuka noa iho. Kāore i pai. I whakamātau ahau ki te tuhi i tētahi mea kia mārama rawa. Āe rā, engari kāore te ao nei e noho mārama ana i ngā wā katoa. Ehara tērā i te mea kāore ia e whakaako, kāore e whakamīharo, kāore e whakakī i te manawa ki te ātaahua me te pōhēhē pai. Nō reira i tuhia e au tētahi pukapuka e kore e haere tika whakamua, engari ka toro whakararo, whakarunga, whakawaho. He pukapuka ka tipu whakapae. Kei te tino whakahīhī ahau ki tēnei mea.

Ko tēnei pukapuka he putanga o ahau anō. Kāore he pukapuka tuarua e tuhia pēnei anō. Kāore hoki ngā momo āhua o au i noho mai i te wā o te tuhituhi e hoki mai i taua raupapa anō. Nō reira ka whakanuia e au aua āhua katoa ki ia whārangi, me ngā āhua katoa kua haere i mua i a rātou. He manapou tēnei, he karanga kia tū, kia neke. He kohinga tuhinga hoahoa mō ngā kaupapa tērā pea ka taea e tātou te mahi ngātahi. Ko te mea nui kē atu, he rauemi ako tēnei, he ara whai mō te kimi tikanga hou rawa atu mō te mahi hanga.

He aha rā te Rhizomatic Design? Ki te kōrero māmā noa, i whānau mai i tētahi pātai paku porangi: ka taea rānei te whakatakoto tahi i ngā ariā a aku tohunga whakaaro tino aroha, arā a Deleuze rāua ko Guattari, ki runga i ngā nekeneke o taku momo hōtaka tino pīrangitia? I tīmata noa iho hei whakaaro whakangahau i ngā wā hōhā. Engari i te kaha o taku huriwhenua i taua whakaaro, ka nui ake tōna tūpono, ka kaha ake tana mahi. Ehara taku i te kī koinei tonu te huarahi e hōtaka ai a Deleuze rāua ko Guattari. Kāo. He kaha ake te tōtika hangarau o tāku. Ā, ehara hoki taku i te kī koinei te rapunga whakaaro ka puta mai i tētahi Data Oriented Design kua whakatangatahia. He whakapoapoa kē tērā. Engari ko tāku kē, ka uru a Deleuze rāua ko Guattari hei taputapu hanga, ka pā mai tētahi momo mākutu. Ka puta tētahi kaha ka taea te whakamahi, i te wā ka whakarērea e DOD ngā mea ehara i a ia, ā, ka titiro kē ki ngā mea ka taea e ia te tū hei mea hou.

Kāore tēnei huarahi e kī ana he whakatinanatanga 1:1 nō Deleuze rāua ko Guattari, nō te rhizome rānei. Engari kua whakaaweawehia ahau e ā rāua mahi toi tahi, pēnei anō me taku whakaaweawehia e ngā kēmu shoot-’em-up a CAVE rāua ko Toaplan. Kāore tērā e tohu ka ngana ahau ki te tārua i a rātou. Ki a au nei, ko te mihi nui rawa a tētahi kaihanga ki tētahi atu kaihanga, ko te whakamahi i ā rātou mahi hei kākano mō tētahi mea tino hou. Tērā pea he āhua korakora, he āhua kōnehunehu tonu ēnei kōrero i tēnei wā, engari ka ū anō tātou ki te whenua mārō. Kāore he whenua āhua noa. He raima waehere. He mea tūturu. Ka ahu atu tātou ki reira.

Mō nāianei, ka taea e au te kī pērā: ko Rhizomatic Design te aro nui ki te tukanga hanga, me te whakatupu i ngā āhua hou ka panoni i te titiro a te tangata ki te ao mō ake tonu atu. He tikanga whakaraupapa e whakakaha ana, kāore e herehere ana. Mā te aro ki te auahatanga hei auahatanga tonu, kaua hei rawa ka tahuna mō te moni a ngā ngeru momona, e whai ana ahau ki te hanga taputapu e whakamana ana i te kaihōtaka hei kaihoahoa hei kaihanga, e āhei ai tātou ki te mahi ngātahi i ngā ara kāore anō kia taea, ā, e puta ai ngā hui auaha kāore rawa tētahi whare AAA e āhei ki te moemoeā.

Goddamn It, Babies. He tangata e hoki tonu ana taku hinengaro ki a ia, ko Kurt Vonnegut. Aroha nui au ki a ia. Ahakoa kāore āna pukapuka i taku tokotoru tino rangatira, ko te āhua o āna tuhinga e whakaniko tahi ana tētahi i tētahi, ka noho ia hei kaituhi tino piri ki taku ngākau. Ko tōna aroha ki te tangata tonu te pūtake o taku piri nui ki a ia.

I mārama rawa ki a Vonnegut kāore te tuhituhi e pā ana ki te whakaatu i tō koi. Engari e pā ana ki te toro atu mā te whārangi, kia pā atu ki te roro o tētahi atu tangata. I tana kōrero “Pity the reader,” ehara i te kōrero whakahīhī iho. He whakaae kē ki te taumaha hinengaro ka whakawhiwhia ki te kaipānui. Ia rerenga kōrero he tono kia wetewetehia ngā tohu matatini, kātahi ka hurihia hei tikanga. He mākutu tērā. Āe, he mahi ngenge hoki.

Ka rerekē rawa te pā mai o tēnei mātauranga ina tuhi koe mō ngā kaupapa hangarau. Kua pānui ahau i te tini o ngā pukapuka “hōhonu” rānei “hangarau” rānei e hari ana ki tō rātou mātao, me te mea nei ko te kore mahana he tohu hōnore. Engari ko Vonnegut? I tuhi ia mō te mutunga o te ao me te ngakau pērā i tētahi kāri “kia ora wawe” tino whai tikanga. Tirohia a Slaughterhouse-Five. I tētahi taumata, he kōrero mō tētahi tangata kua wetekina i te wā, ā, ka kahakina e ngā manene ātea āhua rite ki ngā plungers wharepaku. Engari he kōrero anō mō te mamae, te pakanga, te herekore o te hiahia, me te āhua o te wā anō hoki. Ko te mea whakamīharo, ka taea e Vonnegut te torotoro i ēnei kaupapa taumaha katoa, engari ka noho māmā tonu te pānui, ka kataina, ka ora tonu te kupu.

Ina kī a Vonnegut “Hi ho” rānei, ina tuhi ia i āna whetūriki whakapōrearea, ehara i te āhua whakakatakata noa iho. He tohu kē nā tētahi tangata tūturu kei muri i ngā kupu nei, e ngana ana ki te hono atu ki ētahi atu tangata tūturu. Koinei te take ka waiho tonu e au āku kupu tīkākā, āku pakū kupu, ki roto i tēnei pukapuka. Ko ēnei āku ake whetūriki iti. He tohu nāku e mea ana, “Kei konei ahau, he tangata ora, e ngana ana ki te tuku i tētahi mea e whakapono ana ahau he mea nui.” Kāore au e tuhi pēnei i a Vonnegut. Ka tuhi ia pērā i tētahi Hemingway pāpori. Ka tuhi au pērā i tētahi Pynchon kua whiua e te mate pānui hē.

Ko te akoranga nui rawa i tangohia e au i a Vonnegut ehara i te mea ā-kāhua. He mea ā-kaupapa kē. I roto i Cat’s Cradle ka hanga e ia tētahi whakapono katoa, arā ko Bokononism, e whakaae mārika ana he teka āna akoranga katoa, engari e whakahau tonu ana i te tangata kia whakapono, nā te mea he āwhina kia pai ake te noho. He whakaaro rahi rawa tērā. Ko te tikanga, ehara te tuhituhi, te hōtaka, te hoahoa rānei i te hopu i tētahi pono mutunga kore, engari he hanga taputapu whaihua e āwhina ana i te tangata kia pai ake te noho, te mahi, te auaha.

Koina tonu tā tēnei pukapuka e whai nei kia noho: he kohinga teka whaihua, mēnā rānei e hiahia ana koe ki te kupu huatau ake, he “models”, e āwhina ana pea kia rerekē tō whakaaro mō te hanga. Tērā pea he pono ētahi o ēnei ariā, tērā pea kāo. Engari pēnei i a Bokononism, ko tā rātou whāinga kia nui ake te hari, te ahurei, me te āhua whakahurihuri o te auahatanga.

Kia pono tātou. Kāore au e noho hei kaihōtaka pai rawa atu. I tīmata tōmuri ahau. Kei te whai tonu i ngā hoa iti ake te pakeke i ahau. He uaua ki ahau te akiaki i a au anō kia mahi i ngā mea hōhā. Engari he momo kaiwhakatuma-huri au, pēnei i te rīwai. Kei raro whenua, kāore e haruru, engari ka tini te tupu, ā, ka rite tonu mō te whakatipu i tētahi mea reka, tētahi mea ohorere. I tuhia e au e whā tekau mā toru ngā waiata, mō te marama kotahi noa iho o Akuhata 2024. Ka pakaru mai ngā tikanga hou i te hinganga o te pōtae. He whāiti noa iho ngā wāhanga o taku oranga kua uru ki ēnei whārangi.

Ko te mea tino hira kia mōhiotia, ko taku whakapono nui rawa ki te hanga. Ki ahau, me rapu tātou i ngā mea e pīoioi ai te roro, e tīaho ai te whakaaro, e āwhina ai i a tātou ki te mahi i te mea kotahi e tuku nei i te tūmanako ki ahau mō tātou katoa o te whenua: arā, te hanga.

Ko te tohutohu pai rawa mō te pānui i tēnei pukapuka, he rite tonu ki tā Bill, te kaipupuri toa tawhito o taku toa pukapuka whakamahia tino aroha i Ōtepoti, arā ko Scribes, i kī ai i taku hokonga i taku kape tuatahi o The Crying of Lot 49. I a au e toro atu ana ki te tiki, ka pupuri tonu ia ki tētahi tapa o te pukapuka angiangi, nō reira i pupuri tahi māua i taua pukapuka. Ka titiro mai ia ki oku karu, ka mea mai: “He haerenga tēnei e hoa. Ki te whawhai koe ki a ia, ka kino koe ki a ia.”

Arohaina ō here. I te ao hōtaka, he kaha tonu te whakaaro ko te hua anake te mea rangatira, ahakoa pēhea tō taenga ki reira. Engari kāore tēnei e whai i taua ture. Ko te ariā he mahi. Ko te mahi he ariā. He mea tōrangapū te hōtaka. Ko te kōrero māmā, mēnā ka whakapakokotia tō aronga hōtaka e tētahi tauira tawhito kua utaina mai e ngā rangatira, ā, ka whakawhirinaki taua tauira ki ngā whakaaro hē mō te āhua o te noho hierarchical, ka mau tonu tō hōtaka ki ngā rā o mua.

Ā, tini rawa ngā here. He here hangarau. He here reo. He here rawa. He here mātauranga. He here wā. Engari pēnei i a Guided By Voices i taea te hanga taonga nui rawa atu mā ngā taputapu hopu kōrero pērā i te para, ka taea hoki e tātou te hanga i ngā mea nui i roto tonu i ā tātou here. Ko tā tātou he mōhio ki aua here, me te kite me pēhea rā aua here e whakairo ana i te āhua o ngā mea ka hangaia e tātou.

Nā konei au ka tino kaingākau ki ngā programming paradigms. Ehara ēnei i te kōwhiringa hangarau noa iho. He anga hinengaro ēnei e hanga ana i te āhua o tā tātou titiro ki ngā raruraru me ngā whakautu. Ina whakahaua e tētahi kamupene kia whakamahia te Java me te OOP, ehara i te kōwhiri noa i tētahi stack. Kei te herea e rātou ngā tauira whakaaro e āhei ana ngā kaihanga ki te whakamahi.

Mēnā he rite te reo hōtaka ki te kaituhi i tōna reo tuhituhi, ko te paradigm hōtaka te movement. Pērā i ngā modernist i whai tirohanga ake mō te ao, ka pā ai ki ngā tuhinga i hangaia e rātou, ka pēnā hoki ngā kaihōtaka functional, hei tauira, e whai “ara kite” ake nei ka pā atu ki ngā hōtaka ka tuhia e rātou.

Ko te mea nui, pērā i te linguistic turn i āwhina ai i a tātou ki te mōhio me pēhea te reo e waihanga i te ao, me mātua mōhio hoki tātou me pēhea ngā reo hōtaka me ngā paradigms e waihanga ana i tō tātou ao matihiko. Ehara tēnei i te kōwhiri noa i te taputapu tika mō te mahi. Engari he mōhio ki te āhua e whakatauria ai e ngā taputapu nei ngā momo mahi ka taea rawa e tātou te whakaaro.

Me pēhea te kaipānui e whakatata atu ai? Titiro, e mōhio ana ahau ehara tēnei pukapuka i te mea tikanga noa. Ā, me pēnei tonu mēnā e hiahia ana ahau kia hurihia te āhua o tā tātou whakatata ki te hanga. He kete taputapu tēnei mā te hunga e rongo ana kua herea ngā kēmu me ngā mahi auaha e ngā hanganga tuku iho kua kore e whai hua. E pā ana tēnei ki te whakaaro anō i te hōtaka me te hoahoa kēmu mā tētahi paradigm hou, arā ko Rhizomatic Design, he whakakeke e whakatakoto ana i te whakaputa me te intentional emergence hei mea matua.

E mea atu ana ahau i te tīmatanga tonu: mēnā ka pā mai tētahi mea i roto i ēnei whārangi e āhua rere kē ana, pērā i te peke mai i a Deleuze ki ngā bullet-hell shooters, kaua e māharahara. Kāore koe i ngaro i tētahi whārangi. He mea whiriwhiri tēnei ranunga, kia whakaatu i te pūngao pōkaikaha, ngahau, rhizomatic hoki o te auaha. Ko taku ara tēnei hei whakaatu me pēhea ngā mea e āhua tawhiti ana te tuitui ngātahi. Neke atu i tērā, ehara tēnei pukapuka i te pukapuka noa. He whakaaturanga anō ia nō Rhizomatic Design i a ia anō. Me pārekareka. Me noho hei haerenga.

He reta aroha hoki tēnei ki te hanga, ki te hiringa ake anō hoki. Kei whea kē atu ka kitea he whārangi mō ngā kēmu arcade Hapanihi rūhā e noho tahi ana i te whārangi kotahi me tētahi ao pakiwaitara e nohoia ana e ngā tipu whai whakaaro, me ngā whārangi e kōrero ana mō te rapunga whakaaro Wīwī, ā, ko te aronga nui tonu ko te hōtaka me te hoahoa kēmu? Kei whea ka kite koe i tērā katoa i roto i te kōwae kotahi?

Nō reira, āe, he maha ngā āhua o tēnei pukapuka. He nui rawa pea. Engari i runga ake i ngā mea katoa, he aratohu mahi. He aratohu mō ngā ara hou hei mahi mea, kia kitea ai e koe ō ake ara hou. Ā, mēnā he whakamahi hou tēnā o tētahi tikanga tawhito, i te wā tonu e whakamahia ana, kua noho hei tikanga hou.

Mai i te hanga i tētahi momo hoahoa kēmu hou i runga i tētahi momo kua mau ki nehe, tae noa ki te whakaatu i tētahi tikanga hou mō te whakarite mahi ngātahi ā-ipurangi me te tohatoha rawa mō te katoa i ngā wāhi matihiko, ka haere tēnei pukapuka ki ngā wāhi tawhiti. Kāore pea tōna huarahi e mārama tonu i te wā tonu e pānuihia ana, engari whakapono mai, he take tōna i whārangihia ai pēnei.

Section 1: Ngā Pūtake

Ehara te tīmatanga i te tīmatanga kotahi. He tini ngā pūtake ka rere mai i ngā wāhi kāore e kitea, kāore e taea te tohu hei wāhi kotahi. Ko te rhizome kāore e whakaae kia herea ki te takenga mai kotahi. Ka tipu ia i ngā taha katoa, ā, ka noho ngā tīmatanga katoa hei wāhanga o tētahi rere tonu.
