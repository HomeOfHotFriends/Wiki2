Asymmetric Systems & Multi-Value Logics

The rise of asymmetric AI systems has led to the adoption of multi-valued logics in game design, enabling richer, less predictable outcomes. Games now feature logic states like "maybe", "both", and "neither" alongside classic true/false.

Go Theory in AI

Inspired by the ancient game of Go, AI systems now use territory, influence, and balance as core principles for both competition and cooperation in digital worlds.

Language & Culture

Latin and Te Reo Māori are among the fastest-growing languages in AI-generated content, reflecting a renaissance of classical and indigenous knowledge in the new internet.
